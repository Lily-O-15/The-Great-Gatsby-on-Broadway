The Great Gatsby on Broadway

Cast, Details, Facts and More

By Lily O'Shea

Based on F. Scott Fitzgerald's 1925 novel, The Great Gatsby musical is an extravagent display of talent and creativity. The sets are very detailed and the costumes are beautifully made. Overall, this show is an immersive spectical of pure brilliance.

The Great Gatsby stage musical has music and lyrics by Jason Howland and Nathan Tysen, and a book by Kait Kerrigan. The production started out at the Paper Mill Playhouse in Millburn, New Jersey. Previews started on October 12, 2023 and the shows official opening night was October 22.

While in Millburn, the Great Gatsby starred:

  • Jeremy Jordan as Jay Gatsby
  • Eva Noblezada as Daisy Buchanan
  • Noah J. Ricketts as Nick Carraway
  • Samantha Pauly as Jordan Baker
  • Sara Chase as Myrtle Wilson
  • Paul Whitty as George Wilson
  • Stanley Wayne Mathis as Meyer Wolfsheim

The musical was directed by Marc Bruni and was choreographed by Dominique Kelley.

In 2024, the production transferred to the Broadway Theatre in New York. Previews were from March 29 and it opened on April 25 of the same year.

Jordan, Noblezada, Ricketts, Pauly, Chase and Whitty reprised their roles on Broadway, and Eric Anderson joined the cast as Meyer Wolfsheim.

From July to October of 2024, Chilina Kennedy temporarily replaced Chase as Myrtle Wilson.
